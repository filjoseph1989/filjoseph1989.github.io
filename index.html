Web Developer / Consultant

Company:Code Centris Corporation
Duration:May 2021 - September 2023
Client:Picture Works Group Ltd
Story:

So, I landed on a new opportunity for growth at Code Centris Corporation, a talent provider company in the Philippines, where I worked as a web developer for Picture Works Group's client. My role primarily involved developing an independent service for ZenSmart, a workflow automation software for the printing production business. This service was crucial for integrating new clients with diverse API representations that needed to be translated to the resource structure supported by ZenSmart, which originally came from the legacy system that accepted direct acceptance of transactions as well.

The client payloads could contain multiple transactions, with each transaction having hundreds of orders, and each order can have a number of large images. Additionally, these orders can include special instructions on how the images should be handled or organized, as well as other specific instructions that ZenSmart uses to make decisions when processing these orders. This complexity necessitated a separate service focused on handling the extensive number of images and addressing the special instructions.

My responsibilities included:

  1. Developing RESTful APIs to receive and process client payloads
  2. Analyzing client payloads (JSON or XML format) to extract crucial information
  3. Designing and implementing functionality to transform client data into a format supported by ZenSmart
  4. Producing new payloads that met the main system's requirements
  5. Testing and debugging the RESTful APIs
  6. Collaborating with team members, and and in rare ocation clients to ensure the API met their requirements and specifications
  7. Creating programs to process hundreds of images via FTP, HTTP, or network sharing

During this period, I successfully integrated some of the biggest clients, who sent thousands of transactions per day.

Reason for leaving:

I was laid off from my previous position due to the company's restructuring efforts. In September 2023, several employees, including myself, were affected by layoffs as part of a strategic decision to address operational challenges in some production facilities. Additionally, the company was undergoing a rebranding process and shifting its market focus towards the US. This change in direction led to the restructuring and reduction of workforce, resulting in my departure from the company.

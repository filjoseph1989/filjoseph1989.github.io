ZenSmart's Process Centre

On the right, you are seing the UI of zensmart system.

The Process Center is an independent service running on the server, primarily built using Laravel. Its main responsibility is to act as a middleware service between client systems and zensmart system. The internal system has evolved from a legacy system where existing clients directly sent payloads in a predefined format.

As the system grew and new clients were added, these clients also had predefined payloads that they used to send transaction information. However, these payloads differed from the legacy format. Therefore, the Process Center was built to transcode client payloads and convert them to the legacy payload format used by other systems. Over time, it has also become the entry point for other systems to send transactions.

The Process Center is a powerful service, handling thousands of orders on a daily basis. It is responsible for fetching hundreds of images from different sources defined in the payload. Additionally, it enhances, groups, cuts, rotates, and performs other required operations on the images to prepare them for printing.

I serve as the main developer and point person for this service, responsible for integrating our biggest client through the Process Center. My responsibilities include implementing all requirements and ensuring the service is successfully deployed to production. Additionally, I will work on the ReactJS UI to implement key functionalities that will instruct the service on what actions to perform. I also provide support during production issues, investigating and providing solutions to resolve them promptly.
